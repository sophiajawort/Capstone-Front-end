Data Analyst

Status: Open

Req ID: 14699

Office: Seattle

Job Summary: We are seeking a highly analytical and detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our team. As a Data Analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing large sets of data, identifying trends and insights, and presenting your findings in a clear and concise manner. You will work with a variety of stakeholders within the company, including the marketing, sales, and product development teams. Key Responsibilities:

  • Collect and analyze large sets of data from various sources
  • Identify trends and insights in the data to inform business decisions
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and solve complex problems
  • Develop and maintain data dashboards and reports for stakeholders
  • Communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner
  • Continuously improve data collection and analysis processes to ensure accuracy and efficiency
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in data analysis and visualization
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in a related field such as data science, statistics, or computer science
  • 2+ years of experience in a data analysis role
  • Proficiency in SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Familiarity with statistical analysis and modeling techniques is a plus
If you are a skilled Data Analyst with a passion for driving business success through data-driven insights, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
